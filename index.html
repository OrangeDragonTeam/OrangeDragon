About Project

Welcome to our consulting project, developed by a team of students from Fontys University of Applied Sciences in Eindhoven, currently pursuing the “China & the World Economy” Minor.

With team members fluent in Chinese and equipped with the latest market intelligence, we are prepared to address a variety of real-world business challenges relevant to your company.

Export & Import Strategies with China

We assist businesses in identifying and navigating opportunities to export products and services to China, or to import goods from China. Our focus includes market research, customs procedures, and establishing efficient trade routes that maximize profit and minimize logistical challenges.

Geo-Behavioral Analysis in China

China’s vast geography means consumer behavior can vary significantly by region. We analyze how factors like climate, culture, and economic development in different regions (e.g., Beijing vs. Shenzhen) affect consumer preferences, helping businesses tailor their strategies effectively.

Global Workforce Synergy

We explore best practices for collaboration between employees from different cultural and linguistic backgrounds, ensuring smoother communication and productivity. This includes cross-cultural training programs to help bridge the gap between Chinese and foreign employees.

Culture-Driven Business Strategies

We investigate how organizational culture, leadership styles, and decision-making processes differ in Chinese companies, offering insights into how foreign businesses can adapt their approaches to thrive within Chinese corporate culture.

Sino-Entry Strategy Experts

We provide detailed entry strategies for businesses looking to expand into China. This includes market research, regulatory compliance, partnership strategies, and marketing approaches tailored to specific sectors and regions.

China vs. Europe: Operational Excellence

We offer an in-depth comparison of business processes and strategies between Chinese and European companies, identifying key differences in areas such as innovation, production, decision-making, and human resources management.

Strategic Trade Block Analysis

With trade blocs such as RCEP impacting global business, we monitor emerging trade trends and regulations, offering insights into how businesses can capitalize on or mitigate the effects of these trade policies when dealing with China.

Global Supply Network Management

We design and optimize international supply chains for companies working with or within China. Our services include supplier selection, risk mitigation, logistics management, and cost-efficiency strategies.

Understanding Chinese Consumer Needs

We identify and analyze the specific needs and expectations of Chinese consumers to help businesses develop products and services that resonate with this market. Our research covers demographic trends, purchasing power, and consumer motivations.
